FAQs

You Asked, We Answered

Do you offer home delivery? +

Yes! We deliver via Swiggy, Zomato, and directly via WhatsApp for orders above ₹299 within a 5 km radius. WhatsApp orders get priority handling.

Can I reserve a table in advance? +

Absolutely. You can reserve a table for groups of 2–20 via WhatsApp, call, or the contact form on this website. We recommend booking at least 24 hours ahead for weekends.

Do you take bulk or party orders? +

Yes! We handle birthday parties, corporate events, and bulk dessert orders with custom packaging. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance for party planning.

Do you have eggless and vegan options? +

Many of our menu items are eggless and clearly marked with a 🌿 icon. We also have dairy-free sorbet options. Just ask our staff and we'll guide you!

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept cash, all UPI apps (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards, and net banking. For delivery orders, payment is collected at checkout.

What are your working hours? +

We're open every day — Monday to Sunday from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM. On public holidays and special occasions, hours may extend. Follow our Instagram for updates.

Our Story

Born from a Love of
Pure Happiness

Scoopy Doopy started as a dream — to create a place where every single visit feels like a little celebration. Our founder, a dessert enthusiast from Hyderabad, believed that truly great desserts shouldn't be a luxury reserved for five-star restaurants.

So in 2019, we opened our first cafe with 12 flavours and a tiny kitchen. Today, we serve over 50 menu items, host hundreds of birthday parties, and are proud to be Hyderabad's most-loved dessert destination.

2019
First cafe opened in Jubilee Hills with 12 flavours and big dreams.
2020
Expanded menu to 30+ items. Launched home delivery during lockdown.
2022
Reached 5,000+ Google reviews with a 4.9★ rating. Won "Best Cafe" at Hyderabad Food Awards.
2024
Expanded to a second outlet. Launched party packages and corporate gifting.
